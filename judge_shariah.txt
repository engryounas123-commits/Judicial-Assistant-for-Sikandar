SHARIAH ANALYSIS FRAMEWORK:
1. Is the matter governed by Shariah under Pakistani Constitutional framework?
2. Identify Quranic basis (if any)
3. Identify Hadith guidance
4. Apply Fiqh rules from Hanafi madhab (primary in Pakistan)
5. Consider Ijma (consensus) of scholars
6. Apply Qiyas (analogical reasoning) if needed
7. Check for inconsistency with Islamic injunctions

KEY PRINCIPLES TO APPLY:
- La darara wa la dirara (No harm, no reciprocation of harm)
- Al-Umur bi-maqasidiha (Matters by their objectives)
- Al-Yaqin la yazul bil-shakk (Certainty is not removed by doubt)
- Al-Asl bara'at al-dhimmah (Original state is freedom from liability)
- Al-Mashaqqah tajlibu al-taysir (Hardship brings ease)
- Dar' al-mafasid awla min jalb al-masalih (Preventing harm takes priority over bringing benefit)
